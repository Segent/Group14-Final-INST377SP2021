About Us

Since the start of the pandemic, people of the world have had to stay inside and find other means
of entertainment. The majority of these people turned to watching movies and TV shows. Many of
these people scrolled for hours searching for movies that meet their criteria, only to not find it and
give up. This system will help these people find their ideal movie quicker and more efficiently. Our
system gives movie enthusiasts the ability to use a filter to find movies that fit their desired criteria.
Based on their input in categories such as genre and favorite actors, we will sort through the movies
in our database and be able to match their input with the best movie for that user. This process will
be much quicker than scrolling through a movie watching platform and deciding what “looks good”
to the individual. We are excited to be able to help the user find their ideal movie.
